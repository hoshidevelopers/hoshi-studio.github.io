About

Independent.
Intentional.
Human.

HOSHI STUDIO is an independent iOS app studio based in Seoul, Korea. We build small, focused apps that address the quiet, personal parts of daily life — emotions, habits, reflection, and meaning.

Every app we ship is grounded in a real human need. We don't chase trends. We design experiences that feel considered, calm, and honest — tools you can trust with your inner life.

All apps are built with privacy in mind. Data stays on your device. We don't sell your information. We believe the best apps are the ones you barely notice — they just make your day a little better.
